# E1 — Realistic 500 Facts Benchmark

## Date
2026-05-04

## Hypothesis
System works on diverse real-world facts, not just synthetic templates.

## Method
383 facts across 6 domains: geography, science, history, sports, arts, tech.

## Results
- Average survival: 90.4%
- Min survival: 85.9%
- Post-test: 42/50 (84.0%)

## Verdict
✅ **CONFIRMED** — Realistic diverse facts work at 90% survival.

## Note
Lower than synthetic 95.2% (M300) due to diversity. Acceptable for real-world use.
