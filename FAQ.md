# FAQ

Q: What is WAL?
A: A pre-alpha WeightOps research framework for representing and managing model edits as reproducible artifacts.

Q: How many experiments?
A: 752 Python experiment/prototype scripts, including historical, generated, and cleanup-release scripts.

Q: What is the grade?
A: No release grade. Historical generated reports may contain old grade labels, but current public status is pre-alpha.

Q: Is it ready for production use?
A: No. It is a research-grade pre-alpha prototype.
